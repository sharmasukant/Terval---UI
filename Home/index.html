TRAWLING
WORLD
MEANING

Trawling is a method of fishing that involves pulling a fishing net through
the water behind one or more boats.The net used for trawling is called a trawl.
This principle requires netting bags which are towed through water to catch different
species of fishes or sometimes targeted species.
